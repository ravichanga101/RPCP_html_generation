ALOE VERA

Common name Kumari
Botanical Name Aloe barbadensis
Vernacular name English- Indian Aloe
Hindi-Elya
Gujarati-Eariyo
Sanskrit-Kumarirasasambhava
Family Liliaceae
Class Monocotyledonae
Parts of plant used Leaves
Chemical Constituents Anthraquinone, Glycosides
Therapeutic uses Heals burns. Due to its soothing, moisturizing, and cooling properties, aloe vera is often used to treat burns. Improves digestive health. Promotes oral health. Clears acne and relieves anal fissures.
