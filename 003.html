Asparagus recemosus Willd.

Common name Shatamul
Botanical Name Asparagus recemosus Willd.
Vernacular name English- Asparagus
Hindi-Saytavar
Gujarati-Satavari
Sanskrit-Narayani
Family Liliaceae
Class Magnoliopdida
Parts of plant used Root
Chemical Constituents Sugar, Glycosides, Saponin and Sitosterol
Therapeutic uses Used as herbal medicine and also served as food. All the parts of this plant have therapeutic importance for treatment of stomach ulcer, liver disorders, inflammation, stress-related immune disorders.
